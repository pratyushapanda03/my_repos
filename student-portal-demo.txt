 A Student Portal Application is a web or mobile app built to help students interact with their academic system. It usually provides access to grades, course materials, registration, fees, messaging, and more.

A student portal is a secure website or application where students can:

- View their course schedule

- Check grades and transcripts

- Register for classes

- Communicate with teachers or advisors

- Submit assignments

- Access online learning tools or library resources

- Pay tuition or fees
